The Xiaomi 14 is a flagship smartphone that combines power, elegance, and innovation. Powered by the Snapdragon 8 Gen 3, it delivers top-tier performance with enhanced efficiency. The Leica co-engineered triple 50MP camera system offers professional-grade photography with exceptional clarity and color. It packs a 4,610 mAh battery with blazing-fast 90W wired and 50W wireless charging. Running on Xiaomi’s new HyperOS, the Xiaomi 14 is a compact yet premium flagship built for both speed and sophistication.
